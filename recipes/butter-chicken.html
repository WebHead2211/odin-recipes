Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken, also known as murg makhani, is an Indian curry dish made of chicken thatâ€™s coated in a well seasoned tomato gravy that includes butter and cream.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Whole plain yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Garam masala
  • Fenugreek leaves
  • Smoked paprika
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl toss together chicken pieces, yogurt, lemon juice, 1 Tbsp garam masala, 3 tsp fenugreek leaves, the turmeric, 1/2 tsp smoked paprika, 1 Tbsp garlic, 1 Tbsp ginger and salt to taste (I use 1 tsp).
  2. Move oven rack 3 inches below broiler in oven. Preheat broiler to high heat, once heated place chicken in oven and broil until cooked through and browned, about 10 - 15 minutes*.
  3. Mix chicken in with sauce: Add in cooked chicken pieces and rendered juices from baking sheet.
  4. Heat a few minutes longer: Cover and warm through for a few minutes then serve warm over rice or with naan if desired.
